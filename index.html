About Dr. Sarah Mitchell

With over 15 years of experience as a Registered Dietitian, I've dedicated my career to helping people transform their health through evidence-based nutrition.

My approach combines the latest nutritional science with compassionate, personalized care. I believe that sustainable health changes come from understanding your unique needs, not following one-size-fits-all diets.

I've helped hundreds of clients achieve their goals—from managing chronic conditions like diabetes and heart disease to reaching sustainable weight loss and improving athletic performance.

Credentials & Certifications

  • Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN)
  • Certified Diabetes Care and Education Specialist (CDCES)
  • Master of Science in Clinical Nutrition
  • Board Certified Specialist in Sports Dietetics
  • Certified Intuitive Eating Counselor
